About Us

Wild Circus has been a show business since 1876 and we have kept its authenticity while evolving towards our time over the years.

Keeping its traditional part, we practice the art of the circus for the love that the spectators will want to offer us. It is on these occasions that you will have the opportunity to see the show very closely. If you are lucky, many shows will offer you the possibility to touch the animals...
Wild Circus has evolved over time to offer you a spectacle of qualities, which is why we offer you the best artists of our world. An incredible amount of surprises and magic await you, so do not hesitate, come and have a seat in the big top!

Also we offer you such a huge show that we need a very large family to run the circus. A whole lot of jobs are essential to our success, such as animal care specialists, specialized veterinarians, and also technical jobs, such as sound managers, luminaries, or cleaners... Finally, if you feel you ready to go around the world for one year or more, don't hesitate to contact us by e-mail at contact-job@wildcircus.com, and we will get back to you as soon as possible. For any other informations, mail us directly at infos@wildcircus.com
